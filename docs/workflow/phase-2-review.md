# Phase 2 — Review in Excel

Open the workbook and flip `Include` to `Yes` / `No` as needed.

!!! warning "Save and close the file before Phase 3"
    The build step needs to open the workbook, and Excel's file lock will block it. Save your edits **and close Excel** before moving on.

## What to look for

- **Pre-ticked rows** — these already line up with a canonical SOP control. In most cases you can leave them as `Yes`.
- **Un-ticked rows** — review the **Settings Summary** column to decide whether the policy is relevant for this SOP.
- **Suggested Section** — tells you which Part 2 section the policy will land in (2.1 – 2.7).

## What happens to un-ticked policies?

They're not lost. Anything left as `No` is recorded in **Appendix A — Tenant Policies Not Included in SOP** of the final SOP, grouped by product.
